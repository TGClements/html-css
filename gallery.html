Pictures of My Pepper Plants
Jalapeno

Jalapenos are some of the least hot peppers, but they grow abundantly!

Cayenne

Some Cayenne peppers curl, like this one!

Habanero

On the Scoville scale, Habaneros are the last of the "hot" peppers; any pepper hotter than Habanero is a "superhot" pepper. This one is almost ripe!

Carolina Reaper

The Carolina Reaper currently holds the Guiness World Record for "Hottest Pepper in the World". These plants are still maturing, and will start producing peppers in a few months.
